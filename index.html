Our Sacred Mission

Bible Baptist Church Sinait is dedicated to the faithful preaching of God's Word and the exaltation of our Lord Jesus Christ. Our signature is our commitment to sound doctrine, vibrant worship, and a heart for reaching the lost in our community and beyond. We are a family called to grow in grace and stand firm in the truth of the Scriptures.
